## What are Quick Table Calculations?

These are pre-built formulas that look at the visualization on the screen and calculate values relative to other marks.

**Common uses:**
*   Running Total (Cumulative Sum)
*   Percent of Total ("How much of the pie is this slice?")
*   Year over Year Growth ("How much did we grow since 2023?")
*   Rank ("Who is #1?")

## How to Apply One

1.  **Build your Visual:** Drag `Order Date` onto Columns and `Sales` onto Rows. You see a line chart.
2.  **Right-Click the Measure:** Right-click the green `SUM(Sales)` pill on the Rows shelf.
3.  **Quick Table Calculation:** Select **Quick Table Calculation** form the menu.
4.  **Choose your Calc:** Select **Running Total**.
